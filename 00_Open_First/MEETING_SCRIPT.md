# Meeting Script - External Review Data Center Showcase

## Core message

This package demonstrates a practical path from scattered operational files to a centralized, traceable and refreshable data layer.

## Suggested presentation order

1. Open `OPEN_HERE.html`.
2. Show `Booking Control Board`: commercial and operational visibility in minutes.
3. Show `Capacity / Shortfall`: overbooking, underutilization, cut decisions and commercial alignment.
4. Show `Gate-In / Discharge / Load`: daily operation control and data reliability.
5. Show `Final Moves / All Ports`: port-by-port movement visibility for cost and productivity.
6. Show `BAPLIE / Bayplan 3D`: visual layer replacing slow spreadsheet checks.
7. Show `Telegram Bot`: automated delivery of operational reports to teams.
8. Show `Knowledge Hub`: governance, documentation and single source of reference.

## Strong talking points

- Before: manual checks, several spreadsheets and scattered folders.
- Now: structured input files, standardized Python processing, HTML dashboards and traceability.
- Next step: move the center of the operation to a data layer, with scheduled refreshes and less manual dependency.

## Business impact

- Analysis time reduced from hours to minutes.
- One common view for Commercial, Customer Service, Operations, Planning and the head office.
- Lower risk of wrong-file or outdated-version decisions.
- Clear foundation for scheduled refreshes during the day.
