Context

What this demonstrates

The data already existed. The decisions were already being made. What was missing was a consistent layer between raw system exports and the people who needed to act on them. Every tool shown here was built directly from operational necessity - not as a project, but as a response to a real daily problem. The result is a data operation that runs with fewer manual steps, fewer errors and faster alignment across commercial, planning and documentation.

Commercial - Operations
Booking Control Board
Complete booking visibility by vessel - container mix, over/under status, port breakdown, customer performance, deadlines and pending cargo. Replaced a full morning of manual checks with a single script run used by all departments and overseas offices.

Capacity & Shortfall
Compares commercial forecast against closed bookings - TEU, weight, plugs, shortfall by port and type. Prevented an unnecessary rotation change by exposing discharge concentration risk before the decision was made. Strongest management decision-making tool in the suite.
2h 30 -> 20 min - 950 moves per vessel

BAPLIE / Bayplan 3D Viewer
Reads BAPLIE EDI and Excel load lists and renders the full stowage plan in 3D - with filters by bay, port, status, size and type. Replaced heavy, slow spreadsheet navigation with instant container-level inspection. Started as a prototype, became a recurring daily tool.
