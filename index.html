Background

From Operations to Intelligence

I spent 15 years inside maritime operations, managing bookings, coordinating cargo, planning vessel capacity and making decisions under deadline pressure across Brazilian and South American ports.

At some point I stopped accepting that the answer to every operational question was "wait for the next report." I started building the tools that give you the answer before execution: dashboards, automation pipelines and now AI agents that carry operational memory.

My advantage is not knowing Python. It's knowing exactly what question to ask the data — because I've lived the operation. That context is what most data engineers don't have.
